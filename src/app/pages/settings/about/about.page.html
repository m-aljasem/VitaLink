About VitaLink

VitaLink is a patient-generated health data tracker that allows you to monitor your health metrics and share them securely with your care providers.

Features

  • Track multiple health metrics
  • Share data with care providers
  • View trends and charts
  • Set reminders
  • Export your data

Privacy

Your data is encrypted and stored securely. You control who can see your information through granular sharing settings.
