Born from Love, Built for Everyone

Every innovation starts with a personal story. VitaLink was born from being thousands of miles away from my mother, unable to ensure she was taking medications or monitoring her health.

As a medical professional across continents, I experienced the frustration of distance, language barriers, and complex health apps that exclude those who need them most.

This wasn't just a problem—it was a mission. VitaLink connects families across distances, eliminates language barriers, creates intuitive interfaces, and protects privacy while enabling seamless healthcare sharing.

Today, VitaLink connects families across 9 languages and countless time zones, transforming worry into peace of mind.

Research & Impact

Advancing healthcare through patient-generated health data and multilingual accessibility

Patient-Generated Health Data

VitaLink enables collection of real-world health data (PGHD) for research purposes. Patients can voluntarily contribute anonymized data to advance medical knowledge.

Longitudinal Health Studies

Track health metrics over time to identify patterns, trends, and correlations. Support research in chronic disease management and preventive care.

Global Health Equity

Breaking language barriers enables participation in health research from diverse populations, ensuring more inclusive and representative studies.

Family Health Patterns

Study intergenerational health patterns and family health dynamics across different cultural and geographical contexts.

Privacy-First Research

All research participation is opt-in with full transparency. Data is anonymized and participants maintain complete control over their information.

Research Partnerships

Open to collaborations with academic institutions, healthcare organizations, and research groups interested in patient-generated health data.
